Assignment (09/02/2026)
Assignment Name: AI Around Me

Objective:
	To identify real-life applications of Artificial Intelligence used in daily life and understand the role of Machine Learning behind each application.

Description:
	The following are ten real-life AI applications that are commonly used in day-to-day activities along with a brief explanation of how Machine Learning works in each case.

List of AI Applications:

Google Search Engine
Machine Learning algorithms analyze user queries and past search behavior to provide the most relevant search results.

YouTube Recommendation System
ML models study watch history, likes, and viewing patterns to recommend personalized videos.

Face Recognition in Smartphones
Deep learning techniques are used to extract facial features and match them with stored data for secure authentication.

Google Maps and Navigation Systems
Machine Learning predicts traffic conditions and optimal routes using real-time and historical location data.

Email Spam Detection
ML classifiers learn from previous spam and non-spam emails to automatically filter unwanted messages.

Voice Assistants (Google Assistant, Siri, Alexa)
Speech recognition and Natural Language Processing models interpret voice commands and generate appropriate responses.

Netflix Recommendation System
Machine Learning analyzes viewing history and preferences to suggest movies and TV shows.

Autocorrect and Predictive Text
Language models predict the next word and correct spelling based on commonly used word patterns.

Online Shopping Recommendations
ML algorithms recommend products by analyzing user browsing behavior and purchase history.

Social Media Feeds (Instagram, Facebook)
Machine Learning prioritizes posts in the feed by analyzing user interactions such as likes, shares, and comments.
